The problem

Running a small RIA means
compliance is everyone's second job.

Spreadsheets for annual reviews. Outlook folders for archived emails. A PDF you call your WSP. One SEC exam and the cracks show.

📋

Annual reviews tracked in spreadsheets

Someone's review is overdue. You don't know who. It's not caught until an examiner asks for documentation.

📧

Communications aren't truly archived

SEC Rule 204-2 requires 5-year retention of all client communications. "It's in Outlook" isn't an archive.

⚠️

Compliance is reactive, not proactive

You find out about a KYC issue when you check the file, not 60 days before it becomes a problem.

📱

Advisors are using personal channels

Text messages, WhatsApp, LinkedIn DMs. Client communications you can't see, archive, or monitor.

🗂️

Onboarding takes weeks

Paper forms, wet signatures, back-and-forth emails. A 7-step process that should take 5 days takes 3 weeks.

😰

Every SEC exam is a fire drill

Pulling records, printing emails, building timelines. Two weeks of disruption to answer a 3-day exam.

The solution

Everything your firm needs
to run clean and stay exam-ready.

Core

Client onboarding workflows

7-step digital onboarding: collect info, KYC verification, risk profile, investment agreement signing, ADV Part 2 delivery, suitability review, account opening. Track every client in real time. CCO approval baked in.

  • Client portal — clients complete steps on their phone
  • DocuSign-style agreement signing, no separate tool needed
  • CCO approval gate on suitability and account opening
  • Average completion time: 4.2 days vs. industry average of 18
Compliance

Communication archiving

All client communications across email, SMS, platform messaging, and calls are archived automatically. Immutable, searchable, SEC Rule 204-2 compliant. Pull any record in seconds during an exam.

  • Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace journaling
  • SMS archiving via Twilio
  • Zoom meeting recording + auto-transcription
  • 5-year retention, 2-year immediate access
AI-powered

AI compliance monitoring

Every message, every communication, scanned by AI in real time. Return guarantees, undisclosed conflicts, misleading statements — flagged before they become violations.

  • Flags potential Rule 206(4)-1 violations instantly
  • CCO review queue with one-click resolution
  • AI exam readiness score — 0 to 100
  • Ask AI about any client, workflow, or compliance item
Phase 2

WSP management

Your Written Supervisory Procedures as a living document. Auto-generated from your firm configuration. CCO signs annually. Version history maintained forever. Always current, always audit-ready.

Phase 2

Marketing rule compliance

SEC Rule 206(4)-1 pre-approval workflow. Advisors submit LinkedIn posts, newsletters, and ads. CCO approves or rejects with notes. Full audit trail of every piece of marketing ever published.

Phase 2

Annual review tracking

Automated alerts at 60, 30, and 7 days before each client's annual review is due. Schedule, complete, and document reviews in the platform. Never miss a review again.

Phase 2

Incident & complaint log

Log client complaints, regulatory inquiries, and breach events. Timed response tracking per regulatory requirements. Full timeline and resolution documentation. Ready to produce on demand.

Phase 2

Vendor due diligence

Track custodians, technology providers, and other third parties. Due diligence status, contract expiry, risk ratings, and annual review scheduling — all in one place.
